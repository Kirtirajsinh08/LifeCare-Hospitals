Our Story

LifeCare Hospitals, Ahmedabad

At LifeCare Healthcare, an integral component of the esteemed LifeCare Consortium, we stand as a paramount force in the domain of private healthcare networks throughout the expansive region of Western India. The inception of LifeCare Healthcare has not only reshaped the healthcare panorama in this geographical expanse but has also etched an enduring legacy of unparalleled excellence in the healthcare domain.

LifeCare Healthcare epitomizes pioneering ethos, having established a paradigm that defines industry benchmarks and continually leads the way in setting elevated standards. Presently, the LifeCare Consortium has triumphantly instituted tertiary care multi-specialty hospitals in key cities such as Ahmedabad, Vadodara, and Rajkot, in addition to unveiling a cutting-edge secondary care center in Gandhidham. Noteworthy is the distinction of being the inaugural corporate hospital in Gujarat to garner the prestigious NABH accreditation, underscoring our unwavering dedication to delivering superlative healthcare.

Adhering to its visionary tenets, LifeCare Healthcare has instituted avant-garde units specializing in critical domains, including cardiology and cardiovascular-thoracic surgery, neurology and neurosurgery, nephrology and urology, liver and renal transplant (both live and cadaveric), gastrointestinal medicine and surgery, hematology, bone marrow transplant, oncology and onco-surgery, critical care, emergency and trauma care, orthopedic and joint replacement, spine surgery, obesity surgery, neonatology, as well as general medicine and surgery, among a spectrum of other specialties.
