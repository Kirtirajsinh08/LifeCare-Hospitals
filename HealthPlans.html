Health Check-Up Plans

REGULAR HEALTH CHECKUPS IS EQUAL TO HEALTHY AND WEALTHY LIFE

Selecting LifeCare Hospital for your health checkup is a prudent choice, adhering to the age-old wisdom, "Prevention is better than cure." Our hospital stands out as a premier option, boasting a team of exceptionally skilled and seasoned healthcare professionals who are unwaveringly dedicated to delivering top-notch care to our valued patients. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology, LifeCare ensures accurate diagnoses and effective treatments, aligning with our commitment to the well-being of our community.

Furthermore, LifeCare Hospital provides a diverse array of health checkup packages tailored to accommodate various needs and budgets. These comprehensive packages encompass all essential tests and screenings crucial for assessing overall health and identifying potential issues early on. We also offer additional tests and procedures upon request, ensuring a thorough evaluation and personalized care for each individual.

Beyond our exceptional medical services, we prioritize creating a comfortable and welcoming environment for our patients. Acknowledging the inherent stress associated with hospital visits, LifeCare strives to establish a warm and friendly atmosphere that instills a sense of ease for our visitors.

In essence, entrusting LifeCare Hospital with your health checkup affords you not only peace of mind but also a deeper understanding of your health. Our devoted healthcare professionals are resolute in providing the highest quality care, and we eagerly anticipate the opportunity to welcome you to our esteemed institution.

    Guidelines To Follow During Health Check-Up :-
  • Prior appointment is essential to avoid inconvenience.
  • To schedule an appointment, please call at least 24 hours in advance between 9 am and 8 pm from Monday to Saturday.
  • The reporting time is 8 am to 9 am at the Health Check-up Department of LifeCare Hospital.
  • The entire process takes around 6 hours, but it may vary depending on the selected plan.
  • Please bring all your previous medical records, X-rays, and reports when you come for the check-up.
  • Morning medication is strictly prohibited on the day of the check-up. You can, however, carry your medication along and take it after the blood and urine samples are collected Complimentary breakfast or home sample collection is included free of charge in all packages.
  • Pregnant women should inform the Radiology department before undergoing any tests.
